# ECommerce-Analysis

# ECommerce Performance Analysis using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **ECommerce Performance Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, profitability, and operational metrics.

The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, understand customer segmentation, and make data-driven decisions.

---

# Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Track customer growth and purchasing behavior.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Monitor profit and discount trends.
- Evaluate shipping and delivery performance.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights.

# Dashboard Pages

## KPI Dashboard

Displays overall business performance including:

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Discount
- Average Revenue Per Customer (ARPC)
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Average Items Per Order (AIPO)
- Sales Trend by Year
- Sales by Product Sub Category

---

## Customer & Product Analysis

Provides insights into:

- Sales by Product Category
- Customer Segmentation
- Sales by Region
- Orders by Customer Segment
- Category-wise Performance
- Customer Distribution

---

## Order & Profit Analysis

Analyzes operational performance:

- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Average Delivery Time
- Discount Analysis
- Shipping Method Distribution
- Discount vs Orders

---

# Insights

- Technology category generated the highest sales.
- Standard Shipping is the most preferred shipping method.
- Consumer segment contributes the largest portion of total sales.
- West region records the highest revenue.
- Profitability is influenced by higher discounts.
- Sales show consistent yearly growth.
